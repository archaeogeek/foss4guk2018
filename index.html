Yes but what is it?

OSGeo == Open Source Geospatial Foundation

“a not-for-profit organization whose mission is to foster global adoption of open geospatial technology by being an inclusive software foundation devoted to an open philosophy and participatory community driven development.”

Brief History

Founded in February 2006

First OSGeo-backed FOSS4G conference held in Lausanne, Switzerland in September 2006

OSGeo-Live CD -> DVD -> USB started in 2008

OSGeo UK Chapter had first meeting at Stanstead airport in May 2008

OSGeo:UK held FOSS4G in Nottingham in 2013

OSGeo provides support to the software projects under it's umbrella, and helps foster communities that use and develop it

Pexel CC0

OSGeo:UK provides a UK-based focus and starting point for the work of the global foundation

We organise or enable geospatial events (like this one)

We support development of software by sponsoring projects or specific features

We provide a forum for networking or advice

and generally promote the use of open source geospatial as a viable choice for all types of user

OSGeo is the glue in your open source software stack
